Your current 4 apps + recommended additions

You have: character, item, knowledge, reagent.

I’d add:

campaigns (or world) for Campaign + Party structure

inventory for InventoryEntry + subtypes

(optional) expeditions if that feature will expand

And I’d move Campaign out of the base alchemist app into campaigns. Keep alchemist as the project package + settings only.

Dependency direction (important)

Try to keep dependencies mostly one-way:

reagents → depends on nothing gameplay-specific

items → depends on nothing gameplay-specific

campaigns → depends on accounts (User)

characters → depends on campaigns, items

inventory → depends on characters, campaigns, reagents

knowledge → depends on characters, reagents, campaigns (and maybe inventory)

expeditions → depends on characters, reagents, campaigns

If you follow that, circular import headaches drop massively.

Practical tip: use string FKs everywhere cross-app

In models, do:

models.ForeignKey("characters.Character", ...)

This avoids import cycles and keeps migrations calmer.
